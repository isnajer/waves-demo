WAVES


Isis Najera Theta Waves (4hz - 8hz)

Theta wave pattern activity highlights the interaction that happens between the conscious and nonconscious mind as we think deeply. Research has shown a positive association between Theta waves and memory, creativity, and psychological well-being. Theta brainwaves indicate deep relaxation and occur more frequently in highly experienced meditation practitioners. Theta waves are present in the the hippocampus region of the brain, embedded deep into the temporal lobe.
